Why ALL exists

Mobile shouldn't
cost this much.

Every day, people juggle multiple SIM cards just to get a fair call rate on different networks, top up airtime they didn't plan to spend, and lose money to plans that don't reflect how they actually live. We built ALL because connectivity is a basic need — not a place to overpay.

{# ── Mission / problem statement side ── #}
Our mission

Resolving the real cost of staying connected.

Mobile users across Nigeria pay a hidden tax every month: different rates for different networks, separate SIMs to avoid off-network charges, and data bundles that expire before they're used. ALL was created to remove that tax entirely.

We're building a single platform where one subscription replaces the patchwork of plans, SIMs, and top-ups people currently rely on just to stay reachable.

  • ✓ Transparent pricing — no hidden charges or surprise deductions
  • ✓ One plan that works the same on every network
  • ✓ Built for everyday Nigerians, not just power users
